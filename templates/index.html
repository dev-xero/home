Hi! I'm xero, pronounced: /'zɪrəʊ/. I'm a really curious individual and I like creating things. Usually that takes the form of software.

My interests revolve around a lot of things, some of them include programming/computers, fine art, metal/classical music/or both! And chess. And Japanese anime.

I'm currently a 3rd year computer science student and my experience developing software is mostly on the web. Most of the time, this is backend systems and/or infrastructure.

I spend a significant portion of my time writing code. And since I believe in having passion for your craft, I often put in a lot of effort and attention to detail in matters of interest to me. Though, this means that I tend to have perfectionist tendencies, so when I start a bunch of side projects and start losing interest, it stays there untouched for a very long time.

Since you're still here, I can perhaps entertain (or bore you) with a little bit more about myself. I'm usually asked "what kind of programming do you do?", for a while I didn't have a good answer to this because I do everything. Frontend, Backend, Mobile Native, Tooling, Machine Learning, Graphics et cetera. This does have obvious drawbacks such as never knowing any one field deeply enough. But these days I tend to focus on backend and machine learning tasks.
